Welcome to Virtual Café - NEOLAIA

The virtual cafés are intended to serve as informal, online meeting spaces where members can gather to share ideas, network, and collaborate in a relaxed, casual environment. These virtual cafés provide a space for spontaneous interactions, fostering deeper connections and more dynamic exchanges among community members. This space will be dedicated to researchers, university staff and academics, who will be grouped into "communities of interest" based on topics, functions, or categories.

This service is managed by the University of Salerno.
